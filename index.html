  • Eagles

    Eagles are amazing. Eagles love the storm. When clouds gather, the eagles get excited. The eagle uses the storm’s wind to lift itself higher. Once it finds the wind of the storm, the eagles uses the raging storm to lift itself above the clouds. This gives the eagle an opportunity to glide and rest its wings. In the meantime, all the other birds hide in the leaves and branches of the trees. We can use the storms of life to rise to greater heights. Achievers relish challenges and use them profitably. Eagles have strong vision, which focuses up to 5 kilometers from the air. When an eagle sites prey- even a rodent from this distance, it narrows its focus on it and sets out to get it. No matter the obstacle, the eagle will not move its focus from the prey until it grabs it. Have a vision and remain focused no matter what the obstacle and you will succeed.
